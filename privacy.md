# Privacy

## Privacy Policy
<div class="typewriter">
Fretboard Calculator will NEVER collect any data which can be used identify you, nor any data you generate.  Fretboard Calculator will not even track which musical scales you enjoy nor your favorite keys.  Fretboard Calculator does not contain cookies or tracking pixels.  Fretboard Calculator will not take pictures of you and put them in a database.  Fretboard computer does not track your location and sell it to 3rd parties.  Fretboard Caclulator will not use your musical desires against you nor knowlingly manipulate your emotions in order to advertize to you.  Fretboard Calculator will not monitor you by proxy by asking your friends to share their Contact Book.  Fretboard Calculator will not prey on your insecurities which correspond to harmonic preferences.  If you email Fretboard Calculator, your contact information and all communication history will be deleted upon request.
</div>


## GDPR
This application is compliant with General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).  No user data is collected so no user data collection consent form is neccessary.   Users in the EU have the right to access or request deletion of any data collected even though none is collected.  Unfortunately users in the United States have no GDPR laws or equivalent at the federal level.  If you live in a state that cares about your natural data protection rights, then all state privacy laws will be honored.
